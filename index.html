From the first welcome to the last bite, our team focuses on relaxed hospitality, balanced flavors, and consistent quality.

Nyama Choma Special

Our signature grilled meat experience featuring premium cuts seasoned with traditional Kenyan spices.

Slow-roasted over charcoal and served with ugali, sukuma wiki, and fresh tomato and onion salad, paired with our house special chimichurri sauce.

Samaki na Ugali

Fresh tilapia and perch sourced from Kenya's finest waters, grilled to perfection with aromatic spices.

Our seafood special is served with creamed corn, steamed vegetables, and a tangy lemon-cilantro sauce that brings out the delicate flavors of the fish.

Irio and Githeri Festival

Celebrating Kenya's beloved comfort food classics prepared with organic local ingredients and traditional methods.

Our featured dishes showcase the best of Kenyan comfort cuisine. Irio - a traditional blend of mashed potatoes, corn, and beans - served with succulent grilled meats. Githeri, a hearty combination of corn and beans cooked with onions and tomatoes, offers authentic flavors that have warmed Kenyan families for generations.

Chef's Mandazi and Chapati Selection

Traditional Kenyan breads crafted fresh each morning by our skilled pastry team.

Enjoy our signature mandazi - golden fried dough treats - and perfectly layered chapati served warm with your choice of accompaniments. These timeless treats pair beautifully with our traditional soups, sukuma wiki, and bean stews for an authentic Kenyan breakfast or lunch experience.

Seasonal Vegetable Delights

A vibrant celebration of Kenya's abundant produce, prepared with care and creativity.

Our seasonal vegetable dishes showcase the freshest produce from local Kenyan farmers. From silky sukuma wiki and roasted vegetables to innovative salads featuring tropical fruits, each dish is designed to complement our main courses while standing beautifully on its own for our vegetarian guests.
